    • Measuring Undergraduate Understanding in Computer Science In my EDUC485: Multimedia Literacy course, we were tasked with creating a research paper catered to our educational technology interests. I found that project-based assessments are typically more successful than exams as they provide more room for developer growth and incentivize student interests.
